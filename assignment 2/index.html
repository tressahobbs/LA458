Confirmed Cases of COVID-19 (Novel Coronavirus) in the United States of America




This map shows the number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 in each state as well as the confirmed deaths from the virus and their locations.

This map project focuses on displaying COVID-19 case data on a per-state basis as a choropleth map. In addition to this layer, a point layer identifying the locations of cities where deaths from COVID-19 have occured are displayed as a vector point layer.
