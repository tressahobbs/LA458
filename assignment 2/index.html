Confirmed Cases of COVID-19 (Novel Coronavirus) in the United States of America




This map shows the number of confirmed cases of COVID-19 in each state as well as the confirmed deaths from the virus and their locations.



This map project focuses on displaying COVID-19 case data on a per-state basis as a choropleth map as of March 10th, 2020. In addition to this layer, a vector point layer identifying the confirmed cases of COVID-19 as of March 10th, 2020 is also present. Finally, a heatmap showing the concentration of confirmed COVID-19 cases is also avaliable when the choropleth map, state boundaries, and confirmed cases layers are turned off. This project makes use of data from the Johns Hopkins Center for Systems Science and Engineering and the World Health Organizations.
